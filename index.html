FitAI — AI-Powered Fitness Coach SaaS

A full-stack fitness tracking SaaS app with a personal AI coach. Users can log workouts, track strength progress, beat personal records, and chat with an AI that knows their entire training history. The AI coach uses streaming responses via the Grok API and can generate custom multi-week workout programs from a plain-text description of your goal. Also features AI-powered nutrition logging — describe a meal, get macro estimates instantly.
